My Fave Anime: Gintama

Why Gintama?

Gintama is an anime that combines action, sci-fi, comedy, and drama in one place. Each episode is filled with feels and laughs. Being self-aware, many of it's funniest episodes involve parodies of some other well-known anime. Gintama has had many chapters of its manga and its anime is ending with a movie in 2021.
